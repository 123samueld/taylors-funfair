Cookie Policy Terms

The Taylors Funfair website, like most websites, uses HTTP Cookies. By using this service, you consent to the use of cookies. Our Cookies Policy explains what cookies are, how we use cookies and some further info about cookies.

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files sent from the website to your computer ( your browser actually ). A HTTP Cookie file is stored on your computer and allows the website to recognize you and make your next visit easier.

How This Website Uses Cookies

The Taylors Funfair website only uses cookies to help load the page faster. There are no analytic tools, advertising or ID tracking cookies being used by this website. There are no third party cookies being used by this website either.
