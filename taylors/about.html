Taylor's of Edinburgh Funfairs have been thrilling the public for over 6 generations with their own brand of Fairground entertainment. From the latest in hi-tech, white knuckle rides to the famous Fairground Attraction Waltzer, the Taylor Family have become one of the most respected family's still active in the amusement and mobile catering industry.


All rides and attractions are available for hire for fairs, galas, carnivals, TV/film, private and corporate functions and all can be on site, erected by our fully qualified team of staff within hours of arrival.


Hot food and ponchos are available for days when the weather is wet.

As expected of todays exceptionally high health and safety requirements, all our equipment carries all relevevant certificates, ensuring the very highest standard in customer service. All equipments also carries it's own public liability insurance. We also provide SIA licensed security staff, security fencing, metal roadway track & on-site toilets at all fairs.


If you don't see what you require on this site, please contact us and we will be more than happy to help. Taylors of Edinburgh are members of The Showmens Guild of Great Britain.
